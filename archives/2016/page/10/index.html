Quick Chili

Chili is the vegetarian staple, obviously. Unfortuntely, the “Carne” in Chili con carne literally means meat (in Spanish? I think), so it does take meat to make a legitimate chili.

Ingredients

  • 500g diced tomatoes
  • 250g any mixture of beans
  • 100g ground beef (optional)
  • 100g chopped onions (optional)
  • 25g chili
  • 5g spices
  • 5g salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. If ground beef is used, brown it in a pan.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a pot.
  3. Boil in low heat for two hours, or until the mixture is no longer watery.
