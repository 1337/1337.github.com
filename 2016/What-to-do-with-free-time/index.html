What to Do With Free Time

This list may be ordered by difficulty.

  • Read anything.
  • Go for a walk.
  • Work out.
  • Learn to cook.
  • Do puzzles, riddles, or other forms of exercises.
  • Learn another language.
  • Geocaching. (Might be easier if already going for a walk)
  • Play board games with people genuinely enthusiastic about winning.
  • Learn how to invest money.
  • Volunteer at a local animal shelter.
  • Learn construction.
  • Learn an instrument.
  • Attend a community college night class.
  • Learn mathematics. (Might be easier if already attending night class)
  • Delete Facebook.
  • Travel.
  • Become president and/or prime minister of own country.
