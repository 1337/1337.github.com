Living Alone: Pros and Cons, Volume 2

Pros

  1. No one else will sing.
  2. Set off the smoke alarm, and you are the only person affected.
  3. You can pee at night.
  4. Your location is much easier to insure.
  5. If someone shows up to your door about a food delivery, it is always yours.
  6. If you get sick, only you get sick.

Cons

  1. You have no option to live any farther from the bathroom than you already do.
  2. If you get sick, only you get sick.
  3. You cannot blame a clogged toilet on anyone else.
