Bathroom Etiquette for Men

  • Walk in. Do your business. Walk out. Do not loiter.
  • Do not take out your phone. If it rings, reject the call.
  • Keep your lips closed, no exceptions. Not even if your boss walks in.
  • Face forward at all times. Do not face other people’s watches.
  • If someone you know comes in, either do a light nod, or pretend it does not exist.
  • Do your big business in the innermost available stall.
  • Use a urinal next to someone else’s only as a last resort.
  • Wash hands properly. Wetting hands does not count as washing.
