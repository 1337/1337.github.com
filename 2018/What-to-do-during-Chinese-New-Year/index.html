What to Do During Chinese New Year

Date Northern China Meaning Southern China Meaning
二十三 糖瓜儿粘 糖瓜 is a kind of maltose candy used as a sacrifice to the kitchen god, 灶神.
二十四 扫房子 You clean the house.
二十五 磨豆腐 You make home-Made tofu.
二十六 炖猪肉
二十七 宰公鸡 You kill a chicken.
二十八 把面儿发 You kneed a dough. 洗邋遢 You clean the house.
二十九 蒸馒头 You go and steam buns. 貼春聯 You stick red labels above your door.
三十晚 熬一宵 You stay up until midnight with your family (“守岁”). Also, you “watch and make fun of 春晚 (CCTV new year gala”. 食團年飯 You eat with your family.
大年初一 扭一扭 You do a dance called 扭秧歌. 拜年 You literally wish people happy new year. You also don’t shower or wash your hair.
大年初二 满街走 I guess you walk on the street. 回娘家 Women go back to their parents’ house.
大年初三 上坟(河北) You go to your ancestors’ graves and celebrate with them. 赤口/赤狗 You don’t leave the house today (不拜年).
大年初四 “Rest of new year time you just relax and lay back and do nothing lol” 接神 Somehow you welcome back the spirits that went to heaven since 二十四 last year.
大年初五 開祭財神 You clean up and stop celebrating.
大年初六 啟市 Stores re-open (assuming they close).
大年初七 人日節 On the seventh day (coincidentally) God created humans, so on this day you eat 及第粥.
大年初八 顺星节 Lots of stars today. Presumably you look at stars.
正月初九 天公生 (四川) The birthday of a God. The water you used to wash your face in the morning cannot be spilled onto the ground.
大年初十 忌动石器 You don’t move stones.
正月十一
正月十二 藏剪刀 You put away your scissors.
正月十三 开始上灯 ?
正月十四 拜临水娘娘 If you are a woman, you go worship some water goddess.
正月十五 元宵 You eat 湯圓, 猜燈謎, and, if you are an unwed woman, leave the house. 元宵 Same.

Northern China also has a thing called 小年, which happens on either 二十三 or 二十四.
