Reusable Insults

  1. It looks like everyone on stage had a good time: No one in the audience enjoy this show.
  2. They’re just a free spirit: They make bad decisions.
  3. I love that you don’t care what people think of you: You don’t care what people think of you.
  4. You are “sufficient”: You aren’t the best.
  5. Wow, that was…something!: WTF.
  6. You really did your thing!: You’re a constant failure.
  7. That was so… you: I expected you to be a failure.
  8. Now THAT’S a baby: Your baby has no good traits.
  9. You are such an adventurous dresser: You are a terrible dresser.
  10. I love what you tried to do with X: You suck at doing X.
  11. Good thing you are pretty: Because you sure ain’t smart.
  12. On top of the Bell-curve: You are average.
  13. You so beautiful that you could be a part time model: You aren’t pretty enough to be a real model.
  14. I’m surprised I’m attracted to you: You aren’t attractive.
  15. You’re so handsome now: You weren’t handsome.
  16. Yours is perfect, I don’t like them big anyway: You have no boobs.
  17. You look so pretty/so much better without your glasses: You look ugly with your glasses.
  18. I hope the rest of your day is as pleasant as you are: You are unpleasant.
  19. You seem to do a great job with insert disorganised clients name here because you are so alike: You two are equally despicable.
  20. Bless your heart: Fuck you, or You’re a moron.
  21. You know, it’s not true what people say about you. You’re alright.: People say you are not alright.
  22. That’s a really great picture of you! It doesn’t look like you at all: You don’t look great.
  23. It’s impossible to underestimate you: I don’t estimate you.
  24. You speak really good English for an Asian person: I think poorly of Asian people.
  25. You look great for your age: You are old.
  26. You’re beautiful when you smile: You are ugly other times.
  27. That’s actually a good idea: I’m surprised you aren’t an idiot.
  28. Why are you single: Whats wrong with you that isn’t obvious at the moment?
  29. Well, I don’t hate it: I hate it.
  30. Your daughter is beautiful: You aren’t.
  31. You do you: Go fuck yourself.
  32. You have never been known to use a word that might send a reader to the dictionary: You don’t know words.
  33. I’ve had a perfectly wonderful evening. But this wasn’t it: My evening was ruined.
  34. Bring a friend… if you have one: I don’t think you have friends.
  35. I feel so miserable without you; it’s almost like having you here: You make my life miserable.
  36. He is a self-made man and worships his creator: His ego is unbearable.
  37. There’s nothing wrong with you that reincarnation won’t cure: You are terrible; I hope you die.
  38. You have the attention span of a lightning bolt: You have no attention span.
  39. [You] never open [your mouth] without subtracting from the sum of human knowledge: You’re an idiot, and you’re turning everyone around you into idiots.
  40. In order to avoid being called a flirt, she always yielded easily: She’s a whore.
  41. [You have] Van Gogh’s ear for music: Van Gogh doesn’t have an ear.
  42. Some cause happiness wherever they go; others, whenever they go: Your presence is actively causing misery.
  43. You’ve obviously saved a fortune on clothes: You dress poorly.
  44. Wow I didn’t think you knew this many people: I thought you have no friends.
  45. I hope your mother lives a long life: I hope your mother outlives you.
  46. You cook almost as well as my mother: You can’t cook.
  47. At least sex is not painful with you: Your penis is tiny.
  48. Hm your hair looks so different: Can’t think of a good way to describe your new haircut.
  49. Thank you for your opinion: I have disregarded your opinion.
  50. Each day with you is better than the next: You’re making my life worse, every single day.
  51. Your brain is spanking brand new and shiny: Maybe it’s time you used it.
  52. You don’t sound black: I expected you to sound worse.
  53. You’d make a great lawyer: You talk shit all the time.
  54. You have unlimited potential to improve: You are bad at everything.
  55. You make others feel better about their appearance: Your appearance is among the worst I have seen.
  56. I like your mother in law much more than I do mine: I hate your side of the family.
  57. This is a good attempt at a first draft: Your writing is terrible.
