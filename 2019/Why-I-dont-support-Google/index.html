Why I don't support Google

(Actually just another Pixel 4 rant)

  1. Making fun of Apple for removing the headphone jack, and then removing it themselves.
  2. Removing the headphone jack “to fit in a bigger battery”, and then, in 2019, making the battery smaller.
  3. Dark themes having a dozen different implementations across different first-party apps.
  4. Removing free earbuds and headphone jack adapters from their newer offerings.
  5. Not implementing 4K60 video recording because “it takes up too much space”.
  6. Not including expandable storage because “no one needs that much space”.
  7. After all that, removing free original quality image uploads and charging you for cloud storage, because, clearly, you’ll need that space.
  8. Integrating a telephoto lens instead of a wide-angle because “we think it is more important”.
  9. Making gestures that break navigation drawers.
  10. Removing the perfectly-functional fingerprint scanner because a) they need to shove the radar tech down your throat now that they’ve spent so much money on it, and b) Apple did it first.
  11. Also removing face unlock from everyone else’s existing Android phones because, again, they need ROI on that radar thing somehow.
  12. Disabling USB-C display out because they needed to sell Chromecast devices.

All of that for 800 USD to start.
