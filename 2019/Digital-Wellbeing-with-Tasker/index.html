Digital Wellbeing with Tasker

One of Digital Wellbeing‘s feature is to make your apps monochrome when the timer is up. You can achieve something similar on Tasker, minus the timer.

In this case, the screen turns grey when you open a “time-waster” app, such as Reddit. This discourages you from using the phone by desaturating images and videos that may otherwise look pleasant, while still allowing you to see colour when it is important, for example: in camera and gallery apps.
