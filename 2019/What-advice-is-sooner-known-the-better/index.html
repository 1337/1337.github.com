What advice is sooner known the better?

Source (which is itself a copy from elsewhere)

Never:

  1. Lay with a friend.
  2. Marry a lover??
  3. Mistake your coworkers as your friends.
  4. Work for your friends.
  5. Speak your mind in front of your boss.
  6. Believe your boss’es promises.
  7. Put your emotions on your face.
  8. Talk loudly on the phone.
  9. Give yourself excuses.
  10. (Something about girls and cars)
  11. Believe it when your girl[friend] says “No”.
  12. Expect your ex to change her mind / come back.
  13. Be the third person in a relationship.
  14. Hang out with people who lie to you more than twice.
  15. Lend money from friends.
  16. Badmouth others.

The bottom line:

  1. For people who treat you as nothing, treat them likewise.
  2. Never let anyone think you are really stupid (instead of pretending).
  3. Do not confuse a stiff upper lip with your bottom line.
  4. You do not need to be mad all the time to know that you can be.
  5. Be okay with any truth.

Remember these to make you five years more mature

  1. If you don’t like your job, either quit or shut up.
  2. Learn to accept loneliness.
  3. Be emotionally strong. Do not be hurt by others.
  4. Mind your own words.
  5. ????
  6. If no one calls you, you should be calling.
  7. Don’t get married just because you need to.
  8. Write down what you want to finish in your life. Put it beside your bed. Read it everyday.

What successful people do

  1. Smile.
  2. Be down to earth.
  3. Say good things about people behind their backs.
  4. Tell others little (but not nothing) about their past.
  5. Respect their haters.
  6. Act judicially when it comes to things, but emotionally when it comes to people.
  7. Criticise themselves.
  8. Cheer for others.
  9. Be thankful.
  10. Learn to listen.
  11. Use “we”, not “I”.
  12. Speak little.
  13. Love themselves.

How to maintain relationships

  1. Learn to step in someone’s shoes.
  2. Learn to adapt to any environment.
  3. Be generous / have a big heart.
  4. Be low-key.
  5. Say things people like to hear.
  6. Be polite.
  7. Learn that the more you say, the more mistakes you make.
  8. Learn to give thanks.
  9. Be on time.
  10. Be true to your word.
  11. Learn to be tolerant.
  12. Be a realist. Try your best.
  13. Learn to praise others.
  14. Respect your elders. Understand your subordinates.
  15. Introspect.

What young people need to know

  1. No one but yourself will be brave for you.
  2. If you don’t have an umbrella, you need to run faster.
  3. Whatever path you choose, finish walking it or die trying.

  5. ????
